NBA Player Archetype Analysis

Traditional NBA position labels no longer capture players’ true roles. This project uses clustering on in-game stats to group players into new archetypes; like stretch bigs, inside playmakers, or rebounders. By analyzing these groups, we can better understand team play styles, identify strengths and weaknesses, and suggest ways teams might improve their roster composition.
